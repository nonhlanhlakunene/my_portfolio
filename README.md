## Challenges Faced
One of my biggest challenges was deciding on the overall design direction 
for my portfolio. Choosing a background pattern that added visual depth 
without being distracting was difficult, as was structuring the project 
cards in a clean and consistent way.

## How I Overcame It
I experimented with different subtle vector patterns until I found one 
that complemented my colour palette. For the project cards I focused on 
getting the layout order right — screenshot, tech stack, name, 
description, then buttons.

## What I Learned
I learned that design decisions take just as much thought as writing code. 
Small details like background depth and card structure make a big 
difference to how professional a website looks.
